About Schedule Periods

Schedule periods are predefined date ranges for which you can create employee schedules. Using the schedule builder, you can assign shifts to employees for the entire period.

Workflow
  1. Create a new schedule period
  2. Assign shifts to employees in the schedule builder
  3. Publish the schedule when it's ready
  4. Employees can then view their assigned shifts
Draft vs. Published

Draft schedules are only visible to managers, while published schedules are visible to all employees.
