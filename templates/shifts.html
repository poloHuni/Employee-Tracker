Shift Information

Shifts are templates that define the working hours for your employees. They can be assigned to employees on specific dates through the scheduling system.

Night Shift

When a shift is marked as a night shift, employees assigned to it will receive the night shift allowance for hours worked between 18:00 and 06:00.

Color Coding

Shift colors are used in the scheduling calendar to make it easier to identify different types of shifts.
